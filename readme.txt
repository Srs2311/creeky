TV series information is returned in the following format:
torrent_list_object[torrent[
                        ["series_data{"original_name":"example","overview"......etc}"]
                            [data{}]]]

Where series data is the overall series information, with data being the specific season or episode information, depending on what is extracted from the torrent title

XXX torrent information is returned in the following format:
xxx_torrent_list_object[torrent{data:{""}}]
